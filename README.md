# Digital-Literacy

Digital Literacy Portfolio
Course: CSE0001 - Digital Literacy

University: VIT Bhopal University

📌 Project Overview
This repository contains my comprehensive Digital Literacy Portfolio, developed to demonstrate a practical understanding of digital tools, professional online presence, and cyber safety. The project is divided into five core tasks, each focusing on a key module of the CSE0001 course.

📂 Portfolio Tasks
1. Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Objective: To visually communicate the importance of digital literacy.

Design Tool: Canva.

Description: I created a one-page infographic that breaks down safe internet practices, essential digital tools for students, and tips for maintaining a professional online identity.

2. Student Digital Portfolio
Objective: Establishing a professional digital footprint.

Platforms: LinkedIn, GitHub, and Kaggle.

Description: This task involved setting up professional profiles to showcase academic projects and connect with the global technical and scientific community.

3. Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Objective: Exploring problem-solving and survey tools.

Activities:

Completed a beginner programming challenge on a coding practice platform.

Designed a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms to test knowledge on digital concepts.

🔗 Quiz Link: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1m3Cki8XclCl8_XLuerCAZ_0V_xCNCDve1-Xjf0sIbok/viewform?edit_requested=true#responses

4. Professional Communication & Etiquette
Objective: Mastering formal communication and social media behavior.

Content:

Email 1: Formal request for an assignment extension.

Email 2: Expression of interest for a summer internship.

Social Media Checklist: A set of responsible practices for college students in India.

5. Cybercrime Awareness
Objective: Understanding and preventing digital threats.

Content:

Research-based case study on a specific cybercrime scenario.

“Stay Safe Online” Checklist featuring 8 actionable prevention tips, including UPI and financial safety.

Reporting Channels: National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and Helpline 1930.

🎯 Conclusion

Through this project, I have gained hands-on experience with modern digital collaboration tools and professional etiquette. It has provided me with the skills to navigate the digital world safely while building a 360-degree view of my technical capabilities for my future career.
